THE MAGIC DRUM

We watched as we gallowed and followed,
We watched as vain vailed to unspring,
We sought spring as varlets,
To a beat of drums that runs and rum
We danced with no regard
Vailling that which stocked our ward
Forward and forward we went
And our wits we left.

With the drums beating 
A tone was set, 
There in a song was sang,
A zealous song
Young so long ago a rhythm,
A rhyme for dancers to march.

A rhyme that entices the soul 
With a Vail to make it whole.
Over and over the beats are laid,
With dancers dancing to the drums.

We have danced in rails
Like zombies, we hunger for brains,
Swiftly on shackles we move as trains,
Laced in chains we are held by rails,
No not one to brake,
All so stool to take.
